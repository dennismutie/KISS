Discover Our Story

Kenya Institute of
Social Sciences.

"Empowering the next generation of transformative leaders through skill-based excellence and moral integrity."

Founded in 2009, the Kenya Institute of Social Sciences (KISS) has evolved into a premier destination for holistic education. We believe that true learning transcends the classroom, integrating technical mastery with the character required to excel in a global landscape.

With a strategic presence across Machakos, Mlolongo, and Malili, we serve as an innovation hub where students from diverse backgrounds converge to turn their passions into professional destinies.

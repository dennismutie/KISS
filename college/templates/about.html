Empowering Communities Through Quality Education & Practical Skills Since Inception

Who We Are

Kenya Institute of Social Sciences (KISS) is a fast-growing, TVETA-registered and NITA-accredited Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) institution. We are dedicated to delivering practical, industry-driven education that transforms lives and builds strong communities.

Located in Machakos Town and Mlolongo, our modern campuses offer a wide range of Diploma, Certificate, Artisan and Short Professional Courses in Social Sciences, Health Sciences, Business Studies, Hospitality, ICT, Engineering, Beauty & Fashion, and more.

At KISS, we live by the motto “My Skills – My Future”. We are more than a college — we are a launchpad for changemakers, skilled professionals and community leaders who make a real impact in Kenya and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a centre of excellence in skills development and social empowerment in Kenya and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality technical and vocational education that produces competent graduates ready for the job market and community service.

Who We Are

The Kenya Institute of Social Sciences (KISS) is a center of excellence in Technical and Vocational Training. We are dedicated to bridging the gap between classroom theory and industry requirements through hands-on, practical education.

As a **TVETA registered** and **NITA accredited** college, we operate under strict quality standards to ensure our graduates are competitive in both local and international job markets. Our dual-campus presence in Machakos and Mlolongo serves as a hub for innovation and skill-building.
